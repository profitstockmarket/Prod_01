Mahanagar Gas Limited

By: Shashi Shekhar 22 Feb 2021

Mahanagar Gas Limited (MGL) is promoted by GAIL (India) Limited and incorporated in 1995. It is one of the leading Natural Gas Distribution Companies in India and provides CNG and PNG services.

It has exclusive rights to distribute PNG (Pipeline Natural Gas) to retail customers homes in Mumbai, Thane and Raigad districts with total population of 20 million and 3 million households of which 1.56 million households having pipeline gas connections.

Gas Sector in India

Natural is recognized as the cleanest fossil fuel and is in high demand in highly populated and sophisticated nations.

As per a study by Union Of Concerned Scientist (www.ucsusa.org), Co2 emission during natural gas combustion in a power plant is 60% less as compared to coal or natural oil global warming gases and 15 to 20 percent less heat-trapping gases than gasoline when burned in today’s typical vehicle India is 3rd largest energy consumer with Natural gas as mere 6% (80-90 MMSCMD) of its energy consumption portfolio. And target is to increase the share of natural gas from 6% to 15% in the total energy mix by 2030. To achieve this, the country needs to increase the current 166 MMSCMD5 per day in gas consumption to 600 MMSCMD by 2030.

This means that Mahanagar Gas is future protected and it has very good growth prospects. Demand is available and is higher than supply and therefore company will continue to grow and provide a healthy CAGR

Financials and Shareholding pattern

Govt of India Backed GAIL is the promotor of MGL owning about 32% stake and additional 10% to a foreign entity.

Insurance and domestic Mutual fund houses have another 12% stake.

Retail investors including HNI has 20.79% , making minority shareholders, an significant force if the retail investor demonstrate unity .

The shareholding pattern reflects a classic large and fundamentally strong company with backing from government,foreign funds, large financial institutions, indicating stabality, consistency and stability in the stock.
