ProfitStockMarket.com RECOMMENDS to BUY Large caps and blue chips in small quantities for minor investors Avoid auto & auto ancillary stocks for current quarter and focus on Banking and Cement, technology and Consumer goods sector

Sectoral index Performance

We find Ultratech Cements, JK Cement, HDFC Bank, ICICI Bank, Punjab national bank Kotak Mahindra bank and RBL bank, India Bulls Housing finance, UPL, Hero Moto Corp as good stocks to invest in current environment with a horizon of 6-12 months for a profit of 5%-12%.

Auto Sector which is 50 % total manufacturing sector in India is reeling under perfect storm. Low demand, along with fear of BS VI stage and implication of electrification thrust by govt for all vehicles by 2025 and near-term issues of high auto insurance and input cost, Big daddy, Maruti has been posting continuous decline in profits for straight 4 quarters now.

Overall Nifty Auto is down 4% in last 3 months and a whopping 25% in a year (as on 30 June 2019). This is the worst performance compared to all other sectors. Today , GST rate on all EVs has been reduced from 12 per cent to 5 per cent and tax on chargers or charging stations for EVs has been slashed from 18 per cent to 5 per cent. This will further dent the sentiments of the auto sector as the capacity, inventory which is available will be not be optimally utilized and auto sector to quickly initiate Capex for new technology with will impact bottom line for long time.

Surprisingly, Media is also performing very badly (down 30% in 1 year). Although it does not hold significant weight age, however in the era of global connectivity and so much media relevance, such performance raises serious question on the sector ability and credibility to deliver returns for investors

Another sector, Pharma is also not doing very well. USFDA has kept all Indian Pharma companies on their toes for last several quarters. Bitter margin pressure, cost competition and US authorities has not let Pharma companies improve their performance. Although the Indian Pharma companies are fundamentally strong, however will not be beneficial to minor investors in 2019.
