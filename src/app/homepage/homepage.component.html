About ProfitStockmarket.com

ProfitStockMarket.com is an project to launch a website to help retail investors with ideas, recommendation and Q&A regarding stocks and its fundamentals. Objective of the project is to Design, Develop and Host a B2C Website over server-less cloud(AWS) architecture that can cater to following Business needs . Accept and display queries dynamically from customer on website Provide feature to Like/Share various segments on the website Upload their personal portfolio as an EXCEL upload Signup email to get regular company newsletter
